# Interview Story

## Problem

Freshers often get messages from fake recruiters who promise direct joining, high salary, and guaranteed placement. They ask for registration fees, training fees, security deposits, or personal documents before any verified interview process.

## Solution

JobShield AI acts like a job safety layer before applying. It checks the job text, recruiter email, salary claim, document request, and payment language. The system gives a risk score, explains the exact red flags, and suggests the next safe action.

I built JobShield AI to solve a real fresher safety problem. Before a student pays money, shares documents, or trusts a recruiter, the app analyzes the job post and gives a scam risk score. It explains why the job is risky and what the candidate should verify next. This makes the project practical, socially useful, and interview-ready.
